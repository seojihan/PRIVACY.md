# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Overview

Postrix is built with privacy in mind. Everything runs entirely in your browser — no data is ever sent to a server, and no account is required.

## Data Collection

Postrix does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. This includes:

- No names, emails, or account information
- No browsing history
- No folder or file names
- No URLs or links you add to the canvas
- No usage analytics or tracking

## Data Storage

All workspace data (folders, links, canvas layout) is stored locally in your own browser. It never leaves your device.

## Exported Files

When you export your workspace as an HTML file, that file is saved directly to your device. Postrix has no access to exported files.

## Third-Party Services

Postrix does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
